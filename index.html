fishing grounds

you're not an audience you're the cast
maybe here I can finally figure stuff out
stuff that's hidden in the real world
that's really why I'm up here after all.

I am the fisherman and this stage is my net
I cast slowly as to not disturb our thought pond
I am fed and chastened by this collusion
And I am starving
Let's go

how to tune a cello


the 2 opponents: magic and darkness
where one exists, the other is not safe and must therefore leave.
darkness cannot directly contribute to the work,
it is the intrinsic desire to overcome or lament darkness, that brings forth the magic
the stronger the dark, the stronger the balancer
